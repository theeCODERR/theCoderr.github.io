    Facilities
    Library

    The library is well maintained with a collection of 4000 books, subscribes to 25 of current affairs magazines and 4 daily newspapers There is a fairly large collection of Reference books

    The school libraries are located centrally in the school to facilitate ease of access. The libraries have a great collection of fiction, comics, non – fiction and some reference books. A wide range of books aim to help children discover the joy of reading and help for their development. There are resource books for Teachers to help them keep updated with new upgradations in Education and their subjects.

    The goal of the school library is to ensure that all members of the school get an equal access to books. Students use the library for their project works as well.

    The library is very spacious. It is equipped with a large number of books, story books, colorful Encyclopedias and informative reference books.

    SPORTS

    THE SCHOOL HAS A SPACIOUS GROUND, WHICH CONTAINS A BASKETBALL COURT AND TABLE TENNIS EQUIPMENT. THAT IS ALSO USED AS A VENUE FOR YOGA PRACTICES, COLLEGE FAIRS AND AS THE VENUE OF THE SUMMER CAMP SESSION.

    At any given time, we can see students playing basketball or badminton or trying their hand at table tennis. The well- experienced Physical teachers are always present to give instructions in all the sports, from Basketball, Football to Badminton and Cricket etc. We have a qualified Physical games expert who trains students for Inter school matches.

    The school has won many competitions and also has organized many inter school matches. We have sent students at good level as well for getting exposure in the field of sports.

    CLASSROOMS

    CLASSROOMS ARE LARGE AND SPACIOUS WITH ABUNDANT NATURAL LIGHT TO PROVIDE THE RIGHT AMBIENCE FOR AN EFFECTIVE LEARNING EXPERIENCE AT SCHOOL.

    A noticeboard is provided with every information which is decorated by both teachers and students for different events throughout the academic year.

    The goal of the school library is to ensure that all members of the school get an equal access to books. Students use the library for their project works as well.

    We provide individual help to each and every students . Counselling of students to raise motivational levels and to improve behavior is an important part of our culture.

    Golden Era Academy aims to provide a challenging and inclusive education with an embedded focus on developing a culture of international mindedness designed to give every student success within an beyond our school.

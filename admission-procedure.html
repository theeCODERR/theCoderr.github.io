    ADMISSION PROCEDURE
    Admission Procedure
    Dear Parent / Gaurdian

    We take admissions of the students at the beginning of the academic year. Those seeking admission should produce the following:-

  • Leaving certificate from the school last attended.
  • The Report card of the child showing academic performance participation in co-curricular activities in the school last attended.
  • The original Municipal birth certificate, if the child has not attended school before.

    • A student cannot be admitted without a school leaving certificate from the school he/ she has has last attended. If he/ she comes from a school outside Rajasthan it has to be countersigned by the Educational officer of the place in which that school is situated.

    Golden Era Academy aims to provide a challenging and inclusive education with an embedded focus on developing a culture of international mindedness designed to give every student success within an beyond our school.
